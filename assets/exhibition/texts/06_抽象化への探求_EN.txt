Season 1: In Search of Abstraction / 
Exhibition Caption:


A. About Ano Building

When we give form to architecture, or when we perceive something, we are always cutting out a part of the world. We cannot deal with everything that is visible to us; space is composed through what we choose and how we relate those things to one another.

As a word to describe this process, we chose "abstraction". As long as we try to create something new in this world, abstraction is unavoidable. And yet, abstraction is often treated as if it were a simplified sign. Is it not possible to abstract a subject without stripping away its information?

In this exhibition, we look back self-critically at the way abstraction has been handled, and attempt to redefine it as a method for making architecture. We want to practice abstraction in order to accept, and even enjoy, the fact that we can only ever understand things in fragments.
