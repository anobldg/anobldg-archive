"Ano bldg. and Yokoyama-cho"

The third booklet, "Ano Building and Yokoyama-cho", describes how Ano Building is designed sincerely, without deviating from the laws and rules of the city, as well as our own way of engaging with the city. This perspective is also one way of seeing Ano Building, and is only a fragment. Observing the elements of the surroundings　is close to discovering the rules and order of the city, and incorporating them into the design.
In that sense, the first and third booklets may be deeply connected.
