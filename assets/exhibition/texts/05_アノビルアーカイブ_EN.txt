Ano bldg Archive / Proposal: About Ano bldg / Opening:


Walking through Nihonbashi Yokoyamacho, the streetscape reveals thick layers of time.
Old, small wooden wholesalers that are still in business today. Eight-story buildings with bold ornamentation, built during a period of economic growth. And, more recently, a large redevelopment complex that has appeared by consolidating these small plots of land.
Fragments of architectural design from buildings with various historical backgrounds appear sporadically in one’s field of vision, then disappear.

By containing fragments that resemble the surrounding buildings, Ano bldg allows us to sense connections once again within a streetscape that had seemed fragmented. It gives meaning to the streetscape, while at the same time emerging as architecture through that very streetscape. This is a proposal for a building that strengthens its own reason for being, and that of its surroundings, by intertwining with the relationships already present in the city.
