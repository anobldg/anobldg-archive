Exhibition as a Medium for Architecture / Exhibition Caption:


"Ano bldg and Yokoyamacho"

The third booklet, "Ano bldg and Yokoyamacho", describes how Ano bldg is designed sincerely, without deviating from the laws and rules of the city, as well as our own way of engaging with the city. This perspective is also one way of seeing Ano bldg, and is only a fragment. Observing the elements of the surroundings
is close to discovering the rules and order of the city, and incorporating them into the design.
In that sense, the first and third booklets may be deeply connected.
