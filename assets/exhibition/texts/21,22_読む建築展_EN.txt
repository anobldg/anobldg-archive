Season 3: Exhibition as a Medium for Architecture / 
Exhibition Caption:


"From Fragments to a Whole"

The first booklet, "From Fragments to a Whole", shows how Ano Building, as a single work of architecture, was planned by being reconstructed through the continuity and interruption of its surroundings, history, and many fragments. Within the booklet, multiple chapters and sections are scattered throughout, and the pages, each changing in size from chapter to chapter, gradually shift out of alignment. By deliberately enlarging and emphasizing the misalignment of paper that occurs when the booklet is side-stitched, the composition expresses a return to the whole while preserving the ambiguity that exists between fragments.
