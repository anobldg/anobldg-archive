Ano bldg Archive / Overview:


The exhibition photographs from "About Ano bldg", shown below, are arranged around the captions that appeared in the exhibition. Each photograph corresponds to the explanation given in its caption. In other words, on this website, the exhibition has been reorganized according to the order of the captions.

The "Ano bldg Archive", however, takes a different approach to compilation. After the exhibition, we refined our proposals and outputs based on the feedback we received from many people. It can also be understood as a re-edited version of the project: one that attempts to clarify meanings we had not yet fully grasped when writing the exhibition captions, such as what it means to think about architecture through models and drawings, and what it means to hold an exhibition for the sake of architecture.

The archive seeks a balance between documenting the exhibition chronologically and editing it in a way that conveys what we were thinking.
