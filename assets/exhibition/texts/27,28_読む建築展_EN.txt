Exhibition as a Medium for Architecture / Exhibition Caption:


"Reading Architecture"

The fourth booklet we arrive at in this way, "Reading Architecture", does not bring Ano bldg together into a conclusion. Rather, it teaches us that what we gain can change by first stepping back, then reading through the pages again, and looking once more at the large model. In order to translate the act of reading into a bodily sensation, we used a paper with a tactile quality for the cover, and selected a paper with a perceptible texture for the body text. The two ribbon bookmarks hold the pages in place while encouraging the act of rereading, allowing interpretation to move back and forth freely.
